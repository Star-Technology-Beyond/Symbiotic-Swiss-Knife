# Electric Charge

The Symbiotic Swiss Knife runs entirely on EU. Every action that would consume durability
instead consumes energy from the power unit.

The unbreakability is preserved from the tool material, an unbreakable inserted tool does
not consume energy.

## Capacity and Tier

The multitool's energy capacity and tier are fixed and do not depend on which tools are
installed. All installed tools draw from the same shared energy pool.

## Recharging

The multitool can be charged in any GregTech charging block or machine that supports its
tier, the same way you would charge any other electric tool.

## Running Out of Energy

When the multitool has no charge remaining:

- Mining speed drops to the default hand speed
- Harvest level drops to zero, preventing it from breaking most blocks
- Attack damage and attack speed drop to zero
- Crafting actions are disabled

The multitool will not break. Recharge it to restore full functionality.
