# Switching Tools

The Symbiotic Swiss Knife can hold multiple GregTech tools at once, and switch between them
on the fly without ever opening your inventory.

## Radial Selector

Press <KeyBind id="key.symbiotic_swiss_knife.multitool_selector" /> to open the radial tool
selector. Hover over the tool you want and release the key or click on the option to switch to 
it instantly.

## Scroll Wheel

Hold shift and scroll the mouse wheel to cycle through your installed tools one at a time.
Scrolling up goes to the previous tool, scrolling down goes to the next.

## Auto Select

Middle-click any block in the world to automatically switch to the best installed tool for
that block, based on your configured [auto-select rules](auto_select_rules.md).

If no rule matches the block, a message will appear in chat letting you know.
